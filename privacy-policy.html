Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Little Panda Adventures ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Little Panda Adventures is designed with children's privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • No personal information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information from users.
  • No accounts: The App does not require user registration or login.
  • Local data only: Game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to any server.

Advertising

The free version of the App displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device information for the purpose of serving ads, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • General location (country/region level, not precise location)
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA), subject to your device settings

We use Google AdMob's child-directed settings to ensure ads shown are appropriate for children. Users who purchase the full version ("Unlock Full Adventure") will have all ads removed permanently.

For more information on how Google handles data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock all story days and remove ads. All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Little Panda Adventures is designed for children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • We do not require any personal information to use the App.
  • Ads displayed are configured for child-directed treatment.
  • The App does not contain social media integration, chat features, or links to external websites accessible by children.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to retain. Local game progress is stored on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements (free version only)
  • Apple StoreKit — for processing in-app purchases

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
